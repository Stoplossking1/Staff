# Staff

Staff is an AI agent built for ecommerce owners who want to grow revenue faster.

## What Staff does

- Finds winning product opportunities using market and demand signals
- Helps optimize storefronts for conversion and average order value
- Surfaces practical, high-impact actions store owners can apply quickly

## Vision

Staff acts like an always-on ecommerce growth teammate: researching opportunities, identifying bottlenecks, and recommending clear next steps to increase sales.
